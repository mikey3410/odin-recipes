Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450 grams) of ground beef or chicken
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning mix
  • 12 taco shells (hard or soft)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce, 1 cup diced tomatoes, 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced onions, 1/2 cup sour cream (optional), 1/2 cup salsa (optional)

Steps for cooking

  1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a skillet over medium heat until fully browned; drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning mix and the recommended amount of water (usually 2/3 cup); simmer for 5-7 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  3. While the meat is cooking, warm the taco shells according to package instructions.
  4. Fill each taco shell with the cooked meat mixture, then top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheddar cheese, and diced onions.
  5. Add sour cream and salsa, if desired, and serve immediately.
