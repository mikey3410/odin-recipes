Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (about 225 grams) of spaghetti
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) of marinara sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps for cooking

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil; add the spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat; add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant (about 1-2 minutes).
  3. Add the marinara sauce to the pan; simmer for 5-7 minutes until heated through.
  4. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the sauce; toss to coat evenly.
  5. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves, if desired.
