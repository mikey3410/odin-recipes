Steak

Ingredients

  • 1 steak (ribeye, sirloin, or your preferred cut)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1-2 tablespoons of oil with a high smoke point (canola or vegetable oil)

Steps for cooking

  1. Let the steak sit at room temperature for 30 minutes and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a heavy skillet over high heat until very hot; add oil and heat until it shimmers.
  3. Sear the steak for 2-3 minutes on one side without moving.
  4. Flip the steak and sear the other side for 2-3 minutes; reduce heat to medium and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes per side (aim for 130Â°F internal temperature).
  5. Remove the steak from the pan, let it rest for 5 minutes, then slice and serve.
